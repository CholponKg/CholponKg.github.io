My Country — Kyrgyzstan

  • I am from sunny Central Asian Country Kyrgyzstan. This beautiful land with untouched nature borders with Kazahstan from North, China from South, Uzbekistan from East and Russia from West. Our ancestors were nomadic tribes who chose the greatest pasturelands during summer and more down mountain valleys during winter. The warrior spirit of Kyrgyz people definetely comes from our ancestors who were not afraid of their invadors and foght till the last drop of their blood.
